What is Climate Change?

Climate Change is a large scale issue having many visible effects on our society today. It is the shift in the average weather of a place over many years. The aim of this site is to explore the effects climate change are having on the world, as well as what we can do to reduce the effects that climate change is having on the world.




What human activities have led to climate change?

Humans are a major cause of climate change, due to activities and results that we are seeing in the world. These include:
- Burning fossil fuels
- Increased Greenhouse Gas Emissions which have led to rising temperatures
- Rising sea levels
- More frequent and more intense drought
- Shrinking mountain glaciers




What can we do to help?

We can play our own part as individuals in helping to reduce the effects of climate change on the areas that we live in. Ways in which we can do this include:
- Eating less meat and dairy
- Flying less
- Walking instead of using cars for short journeys
- Reducing energy use e.g. turn off lights in your home when you do not need to use them




Extreme Weather Events that have happened as a result of Climate Change

Many natural disasters have been a consequence of the long-term results that climate change has produced. These include:
- Floods
- Tropical Storms
- Wildfires
